Hi, we are .clearfix

We're a small web development agency from Manchester, England. We take pride in developing bespoke websites for our customers. We have a team of 6 people with a combined experience of 24 years.

We specialise in hand crafting each pixel, and every character of code we write for your website. Each and every team member at .clearfix are passionate about the web and what we do, which means when we take on your project we're as passionate about it as you are.

If you'd like to discuss your project with a member of our team over a cup of coffee, or event a skype chat then why not contact us and share your vision.
